Lil Brickz is a 250 piece Lil Nouns derivative collection each priced at 0.025 Ξ, with traits inspired by, and based on LEGO pieces you know and love.
Every trait is equally rare and every bit of artwork is CC0 licensed.

Cool pixels are only half of the project — every 5 Lil Brickz sold will fund turning 1 Lil Noun into LEGO form complete with instructions by the amazing builder, TheOrangeBrick!

Holders of at least 1 Lil Brick will have access to a poll where they can vote on which Lil Nouns traits they would like to see a LEGO build for. Is your grail a Lil Unicorn, or maybe a Lil Skilift? You can choose your 10 favourites of every trait slot and see them professionally designed in LEGO form.
Instructions for the builds will be freely available to download for anyone, not just holders.
As of current plans more Lil Brickz held will not mean more votes.

Thank you for reading!
If you have any questions or suggestions, shoot a message on Twitter to @LilBrickzNFT!
